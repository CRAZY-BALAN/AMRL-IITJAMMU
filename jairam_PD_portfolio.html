PhD Dissertation · Sept 2021 – May 2026
Laser Powder Bed Fusion of Thermosets plus Thermoplastic Polymer Composites

Developed novel crosslinkable Phenolic-modified with thermoplastic PA12 and Epoxy-modified with elastomeric TPU powder feedstocks. Experimentally validated Selective Laser Polymerization (SLP) for semi-interpenetrating polymer networks with enhanced thermomechanical properties. Demonstrated a sustainable material-process-structure-property framework for duroplastic TPMS lattice metamaterials targeting automotive, aerospace, energy, and biomedical sectors.
