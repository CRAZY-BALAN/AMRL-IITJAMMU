About & Research Interests

Research Overview

3D Printed Polymer Composites — Mechanical, Tribological & Biomedical Applications

Aysha's research explores the behaviour of 3D printed polymer composites reinforced with carbon fibre and nanomaterials, characterising their mechanical and tribological performance. Her work also extends to bio-fabrication — specifically 3D printing of bio-ink scaffolds for skin scar regeneration — bridging materials science and biomedical engineering.
