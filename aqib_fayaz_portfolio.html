Research Focus

PhD Research

PhD Dissertation · August 2024 – Ongoing

Additive Manufacturing of Advanced Composites for Energy and Fuel Cell Applications

Focused on the design and fabrication of flow field plates, gas diffusion layers (GDLs), and PEM membranes using advanced additive manufacturing techniques. The research enhances electrical conductivity, corrosion resistance, and gas distribution efficiency. Novel composite materials and printing strategies enable lightweight, high-performance components for hydrogen fuel cell vehicles and UAVs, integrating numerical simulation, material characterization, and electrochemical performance evaluation.

M.Tech Dissertation · University of Kashmir

Experimental Investigation of Waste Frying Oil Biodiesel Blends on Compression Ignition Engines

Investigated the performance and emission characteristics of waste frying oil biodiesel blends as an alternative fuel for CI engines. Explored sustainable fuel strategies for reduced emissions and improved engine performance.
