Research Focus

PhD Research

PhD Dissertation · Sept 2021 – Present

Design and Development of Advanced Polymer Composite Materials for Laser-Powder Bed Fusion Additive Manufacturing

Investigating novel thermoset-based polymer composites (TPU/Epoxy, phenolic, polyurethane-modified) engineered for L-PBF-AM. Work integrates in-situ process monitoring, X-ray µCT analysis, and thermomechanical characterization targeting automotive, aerospace, energy, and biomedical sectors.

L-PBF / SLS Curing Kinetics Thermomechanical Testing In-situ Monitoring X-ray µCT
Supervisor
Dr. Rajkumar Velu
Assistant Professor-I, IIT Jammu
Ministry of Education PhD Fellowship
MTech Dissertation

Decision Support System for Additive Manufacturing Process Selection using Hybrid MCDM

Developed a systematic decision support framework integrating Best Worst Method (BWM) for criteria weighting and Proximity Indexed Value (PIV) for ranking of alternative AM processes. Published in Sādhanā (Springer, Q2).
