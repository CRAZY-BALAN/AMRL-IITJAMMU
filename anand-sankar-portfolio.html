PhD Research

PhD Dissertation · 2022 – Present

Additive Manufacturing of Fibre-Reinforced Composites via Fused Filament Fabrication

Investigating continuous carbon fibre-reinforced polymer composites fabricated through co-extrusion FFF processes. Work spans fibre orientation modelling, self-monitoring properties, surface topography, and bio-fabrication of functional scaffolds using bio-gel bioprinting systems.

MTech Dissertation · NIT Calicut

Slicing and Deposition Strategies in FFF for Surface Quality Enhancement of Aerodynamic Parts

Investigated the effect of slicing software parameters on part quality of FFF-based additive manufacturing. Achieved Best Paper Award at AIMTDR 2021 for this work. Graduated with First Class Distinction — 8.57 CGPA.
