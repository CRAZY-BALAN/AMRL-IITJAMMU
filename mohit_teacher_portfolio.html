Research Focus

PhD Research

PhD Dissertation · January 2022 – Present

Design and Development of Prostheses using Multi-Material Additive Manufacturing

Developing patient-specific prosthetic sockets for transtibial and transfemoral amputees using multi-material FFF, functionally graded polymer blends (ABS/TPU, PLA/TPU), stress-field driven lattice structures (Gyroid TPMS), and CFD-based thermal comfort modelling. Integrates co-extrusion nozzle design, FEA simulation, and clinical gait analysis for biomechanical optimisation.
